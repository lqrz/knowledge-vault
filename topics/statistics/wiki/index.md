## Learning Path

- Start with [[probability/probability-distributions|probability-distributions]] and [[probability/conditional-probability|conditional-probability]] to understand the distribution factorizations used later.
- Read [[statistical-paradoxes/simpsons-paradox|simpsons-paradox]] to see why aggregate statistics can reverse after conditioning on a confounder.
- Read [[information-theory/prefix-codes-and-entropy|prefix-codes-and-entropy]] before [[information-theory/entropy|entropy]] to understand entropy as a coding limit.
- Then read [[information-theory/cross-entropy|cross-entropy]] and [[information-theory/kl-divergence|kl-divergence]] to connect coding inefficiency with distribution mismatch.
- Read [[information-theory/joint-and-conditional-entropy|joint-and-conditional-entropy]] and [[information-theory/mutual-information|mutual-information]] to understand shared information between variables.
